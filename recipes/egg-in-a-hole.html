Egg in a Hole

Description

Create a sandwich hole and throw eggs in. Enjoy an egg in a fancy way!

Ingredients

  • 1 Â½ teaspoons bacon grease
  • 1 slice bread
  • 1 egg
  • salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Melt bacon grease in a nonstick pan over low heat.
  2. Cut a 2-inch hole in the center of bread slice; place in the hot skillet and cook until lightly toasted, about 2 minutes.
  3. lip bread and crack egg into the hole; season with salt and pepper.
  4. Continue to cook until egg is cooked and mostly firm; flip again and cook 1 minute more.
  5. Eat IMMEDIATELY!
