Egg Drop Soup

Description

A 15 minutes Chinese Dish! Enjoy Restaurant Quality at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ¼ teaspoon soy sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch (Optional)

Steps

  1. Combine chicken broth, soy sauce, and sesame oil in a small saucepan over medium heat; bring to a boil.
  2. Stir water and cornstarch together in a small bowl until cornstarch is dissolved; pour into boiling broth (optional)
  3. Slowly pour in beaten egg, stirring constantly
  4. Season with white pepper and salt
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh chives.
  6. Eat IMMEDIATELY!
